Les Globes de Cristal (French Arts & Culture Awards) - winners


The 7th edition of Les Globes de Cristal (Crystal Globes) took place in Paris for French Arts and Culture in twelve categories. Actress Carole Bouquet hosted the ceremony

Intouchables by Olivier Nakache and Eric Toledano was named Best Film while Omar Sy was named Best Actor. The film was up against Polisse by Maïwenn, The Artist by Michel Hazanavicius, La Guerre est déclarée (Declaration of War) by Valérie Donzelli and L'Exercice de l‘Etat (The Minister) by Pierre Schoeller. Omar Sy had been competing with his co-star François Cluzet as well as Jean Dujardin (The Artist), Olivier Gourmet L'Exercice de l‘Etat (The Minister), and Jean Pierre Daroussin (Les Neiges du Kilimandjaro (The Snows of Kilimanjaro)).

The Best Actress award went to Karin Viard and Marina Foïs jointly for their work in Maïwenn's Polisse. Their fellow nominees had been Bérénice Béjo (The Artist), Marie Gillain (Toutes nos envies (All Our Desires)), Valérie Donzelli (La Guerre est déclarée (Declaration of War)) and Leïla Bekhti (La Source des femmes (The Source)).

An honourary was given to writer/novelist Eric Reinhardt.

The Award winners were chosen by a jury of 27 journalists headed by Nicolas Demorand.
